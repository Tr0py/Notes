# Jump to Top of Page / Table of Contents

By pressing `Ctrl-[` you can jump to previous location.  Therefore, if you
generate a Table of Contents (TOC) at the very beginning of your page, and jump
to some seciton by clicking the link, you can later get back to your TOC by
pressing `Ctrl-[`.  Moreover, if you want to go forward, use `Ctrl-]`.

Therefore, this achives similar usage as opening / closing sidebar TOC, or jump
to top of page.
